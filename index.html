Work Experience

January 2017 - Present

INRA
Research Scientist

I'm currently working as researcher at the Moroccan National Institute for Agricultural Research (INRA). During my doctoral and postdoctoral stages, I worked on the soft rot pathogens Pectobacterium and Dickeya exploring multiple aspects such as genomics, evolution, virulence and ecology. My current project is focused on the genomics of endemic species Argania spinosa. We are taking advantages of the development of NGS technologies and genome-based approaches to highlight the genome patrimony of this precious species. In this context, we are addressing several issues such as molecular markers for important traits, conservation of the genetic resources.
